About me

Popular Music Technology graduate with a passion for making music, videos and websites. I am fascinated by the technicalities and science behind all things audio and visual.

Video Editing

In 2018 my band needed a lyric video. Looking around the internet I realised they were all very similar and showed little personality. Seeing as I had figured out how to use Photoshop at a young age, how hard could Premiere Pro be? It was time to make my own.


While only having an out of date computer with CS3 I embraced the glitches and used them as a stylistic feature to create my own aesthetic, which bands in the local scene liked. I fell in love with the process and now create videos professionally.

Video Production

After discussing the imagery invoked by the song, I edit footage to sync visual beats with musical beats, creating a mini narrative to accompany the audio with expressive animated text.
